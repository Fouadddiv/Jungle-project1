Jungles are: natural environments dense with trees and plants, rich in biological diversity,

animals,

Lion

The lion is a mammal from the feline family and
is considered one of the largest in the world after the tiger.
The lion is distinguished by its strength and courage, and therefore
it is called the king of the jungle.

elephant

The elephant is: the largest land animal on the face of the earth today.
It is distinguished by its huge size, long trunk, and strong tusks.
There are two main types of elephants:
the African elephant and the Asian elephant.
1 African elephant:
This type of elephant is characterized by many characteristics that make it an amazing creature, including:
Size and strength: The African elephant is the largest land animal on Earth,
distinguished by its huge body and tremendous strength.
Intelligence: African elephants have high intelligence and the ability to learn and remember.
A social animal: African elephants live in groups, forming a strong, cohesive society.

Bear

The bear is: a mammal from the bear family,
characterized by its strength, large size, and thick fur.
The bear lives in different regions of the world, from rain forests to the polar tundra,
and varies in sizes, colors, and types according to the environment in which it lives.
